Graduation Disparities in Massachusetts

This project explores how graduation rates vary across Massachusetts school districts and how those differences may relate to socioeconomic factors.

Project Overview

Graduating from high school is a major milestone, but outcomes are not equal across all communities. Our project examines whether graduation rates differ across Massachusetts districts and how those patterns may connect to factors such as economic disadvantage and student need.
